About us

Copamar Hotel is conveniently located just 300 meters from the famous Copacabana Beach, 2 blocks from Siqueira Campos Metro Station and 20 minutes from the airport, Corcovado and Sugar Loaf. It offers rooms with TV and free Wi-Fi. Some rooms at Hotel Copamar feature a private sauna and a hot tub. All rooms are practical equipped with air conditioning, mini-bar and cable TV. A buffet breakfast is served in the tropical rooms. Include fresh fruits, juices, breads and cakes. The reception can provide information about the sights in Rio de Janeiro. Guests have a variety of options for dining and nightlife around the Copamar Hotel.
